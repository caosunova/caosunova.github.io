Research Areas

My research examines how AI changes what workers do and learn, combining economic modeling with simulations and field experiments in real organizations (ideally).

I'm particularly interested in:

  • Task allocation and skill dynamics under AI adoption
  • Worker-AI interaction and collaboration patterns
  • Organizational learning with artificial agents
  • Economic modeling of technology-driven workplace changes
