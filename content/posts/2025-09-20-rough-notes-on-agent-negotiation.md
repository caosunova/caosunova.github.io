We live in a dangerous and exciting time.

Funny timing: I start writing just as LLMs flood the internet and “writing” becomes a weaker signal. Still, it’s the best way I know to sort out what I think.

Outside my main focus on AI and organizations, I often think about a few bigger questions at the intersection of technology, economics, and society. 

I’ll be putting out some short notes on questions I keep circling. For starters:

- digital worlds and how they reshape life and ambitions
- how tech reshuffles power in America
- where our information and production systems align—or don’t

No theses yet—just sketches I may develop into research ideas later. If I'm going to compulsively about this  in my free time I may as well make something of it.
