It's a strange time to start writing. Large language models have made "writing" a less reliable signal of thought than it used to be. Still, writing remains the best way I know to work through ideas that won't leave me alone.

Outside my main research on AI and organizations, I think a lot about the role that technology and economic systems play in shaping social and political life. Some questions I keep returning to:

  • Digital worlds and how they reshape life and ambition
  • How technology reshuffles power in America
  • Where our information and production systems align — or don't

No theses yet — just sketches that may or may not develop into something more. Book recommendations welcome.
