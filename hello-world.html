September 2025

Starting a writing page in 2025 is a little funny. AI has made writing a worse signal of thought than it used to be, so there's not much reason for anyone to trust that what they're reading here came from a person. I guess you'll have to take my word for it.

I think writing is important anyway. It's the only way to communicate with someone without being in the room, and the process of doing it shapes what you actually think. Most of my half-formed ideas stay half-formed until I try to write them down and realize I don't understand them as well as I thought.

This is where those ideas will go. Mostly things about technology, economics, and how they interact with each other and with people. No promises on frequency or quality.
